About Us

Band History

In a world filled with similar-sounding bands, Midnight Echoes stands out with their unique blend of alternative rock, post-punk revival, and introspective lyrics. Formed in 2010 in Seattle, Washington, the band consists of Alex Turner (vocals and guitar), Jamie Lee (bass), and Sam Carter (drums). Their chemistry as longtime friends and collaborators is evident in their music and performances.

Alex Turner leads the band's creative direction, producing and co-writing their albums, while Jamie Lee and Sam Carter bring their own flair to the live shows. Together, they create a dynamic and engaging experience for their audience, combining thoughtful production with energetic performances.

Their debut album, "Shadows in the Night" (2014), and its hit single "Whispers in the Dark" catapulted them into the spotlight. Their follow-up album, "Eclipsed" (2017), solidified their place in the alternative rock scene with the chart-topping single "Fading Lights." Despite the challenges of the global pandemic, they released "Isolation" (2021), an album that captured the emotional landscape of the times.

With their latest album, "Resurgence" (2024), Midnight Echoes has returned stronger than ever, blending their signature sound with new influences. Their music has resonated with fans and critics alike, amassing millions of streams and earning them a dedicated following.

Currently on a world tour, Midnight Echoes continues to captivate audiences with their evolving music and powerful performances. With over a decade of experience, they remain a dynamic force in the music industry, always pushing the boundaries of their art.
